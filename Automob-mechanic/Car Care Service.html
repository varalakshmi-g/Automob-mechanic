Car Care Service

AutoMob-Mechanic offers comprehensive car care services to keep your vehicle looking its best and performing at its peak. Our skilled technicians use premium products and techniques to provide meticulous care for your car. Our car care services include:

Exterior Detailing

We'll thoroughly clean and polish your vehicle's exterior, removing dirt, grime, and contaminants. Our detailing process includes hand washing, waxing, and polishing to restore shine and protect the paintwork.

Interior Detailing

We'll clean and refresh the interior of your car, ensuring every surface is spotless. Our interior detailing involves vacuuming, steam cleaning, leather conditioning, and fabric protection to enhance comfort and aesthetics.

Headlight Restoration

If your headlights have become dull or yellowed, we can restore their clarity and brightness. Our headlight restoration service improves visibility and enhances the overall appearance of your vehicle.

Wheel and Tire Care

We'll clean your wheels and tires, removing brake dust, dirt, and grime. Additionally, we can apply protective coatings to the wheels to prevent corrosion and keep them looking pristine.

Paint Protection

We offer paint protection services to shield your vehicle's paintwork from environmental hazards and UV rays. Our protective coatings create a barrier that helps maintain the shine and protects against fading and oxidation.

Odor Removal

If your car has unpleasant odors, we can eliminate them using specialized techniques. Our odor removal service targets the source of the odor, leaving your vehicle smelling fresh and clean.
