Body Repair Service

AutoMob-Mechanic offers professional body repair services to restore your vehicle's appearance and structural integrity. Our skilled technicians utilize advanced equipment and industry-approved techniques to deliver exceptional results. Our body repair services include:

Dent and Scratch Removal

We can repair minor dents, scratches, and paint imperfections, restoring the original look of your vehicle. Our technicians use specialized tools and techniques to achieve seamless results.

Panel Replacement

If your vehicle has sustained significant damage to a panel, we can replace it with a high-quality replacement part. We ensure precise fitting and color matching to seamlessly integrate the new panel.

Paintwork and Color Matching

Our expert painters can match your vehicle's original paint color and apply it with precision. Whether it's a small touch-up or a full repaint, we guarantee a flawless and durable finish.

Bumper Repair and Replacement

If your bumper is damaged, we can repair it whenever possible. In cases of severe damage, we offer bumper replacement with genuine parts to restore both aesthetics and safety.

Frame Straightening

In the event of frame damage, we employ advanced techniques to straighten and align the vehicle's frame. This ensures proper weight distribution and maintains the structural integrity of your car.

Glass Replacement
