Preventive Maintenance Service

At AutoMob-Mechanic, we understand the importance of regular maintenance to keep your vehicle running smoothly and prevent costly repairs in the future. Our preventive maintenance service covers a wide range of essential tasks to ensure the optimal performance and longevity of your car:

Oil Change

Regular oil changes are crucial for maintaining the health of your engine. We will replace the old oil and filter, ensuring proper lubrication and reducing the risk of engine damage.

Fluid Checks and Top-ups

We'll inspect and replenish vital fluids such as coolant, brake fluid, power steering fluid, and windshield washer fluid to keep your vehicle's systems running smoothly.

Tire Rotation and Balancing

Properly rotated and balanced tires ensure even wear and extend their lifespan. Our technicians will rotate your tires and check the balance, improving stability and performance.

Brake Inspection

We'll inspect your brake system, including pads, rotors, calipers, and brake lines, to ensure they are in good working condition. Any necessary repairs or replacements will be recommended.

Battery Check

We'll examine your battery's health, clean the terminals, and perform load testing to ensure it can provide reliable starting power. If needed, we'll recommend battery replacement.

Filter Replacements

We'll replace your air filter, cabin air filter, and fuel filter as necessary to maintain proper airflow, improve fuel efficiency, and protect your engine from contaminants.

By investing in our preventive maintenance services, you can enjoy a safer and more reliable driving experience. To schedule a preventive maintenance service or for more information, please contact our team using the details provided on our Contact Us page.
